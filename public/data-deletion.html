Delete Your Data

Your data, your choice

Autonodal gives you full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time using either of the methods below.

Option 1: Account Settings

Self-service

Sign in and navigate to your Account Settings. Under the account management section, select Delete Account. This will immediately begin the deletion process.

Option 2: Email Request

Manual request

Send an email to privacy@autonodal.com from the email address associated with your account. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will process your request and confirm within 5 business days.

What Happens When You Delete

Upon initiating account deletion:

All personal data, proximity graphs, connected account metadata, and signal subscriptions are permanently and irrecoverably deleted within 30 days.

This includes:

  • Your account profile and credentials
  • All proximity scores and connection metadata
  • All connected service data (Gmail, Contacts, Calendar)
  • All signal feed subscriptions and history
  • Any data contributed to Huddles

We NEVER retain identifiable personal data after the 30-day deletion window. Aggregated, anonymised statistics may be retained but cannot be linked back to you.

Questions

If you have any questions about data deletion, contact us at privacy@autonodal.com.
